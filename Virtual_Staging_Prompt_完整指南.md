# Virtual Staging Prompt 完整指南

**五大風格 × 七種空間 完整 Prompt 模板**

保留原始拍攝角度與空間結構，家具可替換為對應風格。

\---

## 使用說明

本文件包含兩種 Prompt 格式：

1. **通用萬用版（Generic）** — 適合整體空間一次性轉換。
2. **空間專用版（Room-Specific）** — 針對不同空間給出精準的材質、色彩、質感指引。

**核心原則：** 拍攝角度與建築結構絕對不變，家具可自由替換為對應風格。

建議搭配 AI 圖片編輯工具（如 Google ImageFX / Gemini、Midjourney、Stable Diffusion）使用。

## 1\. 日式無印風 MUJI / Japandi

**適合：** 小坪數、空房 staging、高質感但不浮誇

* **材質 Materials：** light wood, oak, ash, natural linen, cotton
* **色彩 Colors：** white, beige, light brown, warm grey
* **質感 Textures：** clean, minimal, natural grain, matte finishes
* **氛圍 Atmosphere：** Soft natural lighting, airy space, uncluttered, zen feeling.

### 通用萬用版 Generic Prompt

```
Transform the interior into a Japanese Muji / Japandi style.
Preserve the original architecture exactly: wall positions, windows, doors, ceiling, and spatial structure must remain unchanged.
Wall surface finishes (paint, wallpaper, texture) should be updated to match the target style.
Keep the same camera angle and perspective.
Furniture can be replaced or restyled to match the design language.
Update materials, colors, textures, and decorations consistently across the entire space.
Use realistic materials, proper lighting, and coherent spatial design.
Interior design photography, natural lighting, clean composition, high realism.
```
